OUR STORY

BOGI CARE was launched with the intention of being a baby care brand that cares not only the hygiene of babies, but we also care about the health of mommies and every member in a family.

Moreover, to be different from other, we developed our special and unique formula called “Moisturizing Formula” which helps to preserve mom’s hands without dryness while washing baby bottle, nipple, baby accessories, and baby clothes.

All of our products are free from harmful chemicals such as SLS, Paraben, and Alcohol.

The intention of our brand is being a brand that cares for both mothers and babies that produce quality products at reasonable prices.

For our business customers:
