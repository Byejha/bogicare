BOGI CARE IS FREE FROM SLS

Sodium lauryl sulfate (SLS) is one of the ingredients you'll find listed on your shampoo, soap, dishwashing, cosmetic, and most of household cleaners.

According to the NIH report, SLS concentrations of more than 2% can cause skin irritation. But the concentration of SLS in household cleaning products can vary from 1% to 30%, and in personal care products (like bubble baths and body washes), SLS concentrations can range even higher.
For safety, Babies and Pregnant Women should avoid the products that contain SLS.
